Emelie Sandberg

My journey started when I was 6yrs old. I was introduced to the world of circus and my life changed from that day forward. I have worked around the world as a performer both arial and on skates for over 12 years. One day a friend of mine invited me to a yoga class and that is when my life changed once again. Yoga helped me both mentally and physically overcome pain and stress. When I say pain I'm talking about the pain you get as an athlete from doing the same thing over and over that your body doesn't have enough time to recover. The stretches and exercises that I have learned over the years have helped reduce the pain in many areas completely. Once I witnessed the benefits of yoga I took courses to become certified and made it a goal to help others achieve the same success I have.
